Who we are:

We are a group of students from the Department of Chemical Engineering at the University of Jordan. Our goal is to provide all academic and recreational assistance to the largest number of chemical engineering students, in particular, and engineering students in general, in the best and most effective manner.

Our Vision:

To offer the best academic service and methodology that elevates the skills of chemical engineering students, preparing them to deal with all aspects of the job market. We also aim to raise awareness about the importance of chemical engineering and the prominent role of chemical engineers in advanced industries.

Our Mission:

To provide all academic requirements and recreational activities for chemical engineering students, creating an environment for communication and constructive dialogue among them. We work towards enhancing their capabilities by organizing courses and extracurricular activities in coordination with relevant authorities, especially the Jordanian Engineers Association. Additionally, we aim to discover talents and support individual initiatives.

Our Values:

Creativity, innovation, dedication, commitment.
